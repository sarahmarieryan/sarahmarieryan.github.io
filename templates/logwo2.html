Workout 2: Running + Throwing

Warm Up
10 jog + min dynamic stretching like at the beginning of practice

Sprinting
4 x 200 yds sprint with a 45 second break between sprints
Water break *use water breaks to stretch what you need and to recover so your next sprints will be full speed!
8x 50 yd sprint with 30 second break between sprints
Water break
4 x 50 yd sprint with 15 second break between sprints
Water break
8 x 20 yard sprint with 30 second break between sprints

Throwing
Complete 10 backhands and 10 forehands in a row. However long it takes!
Complete 25 backhands at a farther distance than is comfortable.
Complete 25 forehandss at a farther distance than is comfortable.
Complete 10 backhands and forehands with a lower release than is comfortable (drop your hand lower and release low!)

Cool Down
Stretch/roll what you need
1 minute of front core
Balance 1min/side eyes closed
