Throwing
Complete 10 backhands and 10 forehands in a row. However long it takes!
Complete 25 backhands at a farther distance than is comfortable.
Complete 25 forehandss at a farther distance than is comfortable.
Complete 10 backhands and forehands with a lower release than is comfortable (drop your hand lower and release low!)
Get funky with it! practice all of these throws again but with O/I and I/O angles. If it is windy, try throwing upwind/downwind/crosswind.
Be sure you are taking a step out with your throws and practice some fakes!
