Workout 3: Running, Balance and Core Circuits

Tempo Run
60 seconds run hard (at mile pace or faster), 30 seconds jog (x6)
30 seconds run hard (at 800 meter pace or faster. Almost a sprint), 30 seconds jog (x8)
15 seconds sprint (ALL OUT SPEED!), jog 45 seconds (x8)

Balance Circuit
All exercises run stright into the next, so see if you can keep one foot up the entire time! Balance on 1 leg 30 seconds
50 calf raises on that same leg
Single leg deadlift x6 on the same side
Switch and repeat x 2 each side

Core Circuit
30 seconds crunches
30 seconds plank
30 Seconds mtn climber
Entire set X3 with 30 seconds rest between each set

Cool Down
5 min dynamic stretching, rolling, or yoga
Do not stop skip stretching, it will lead to tightness and injury
